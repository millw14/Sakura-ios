# Privacy Policy

**Sakura**
Last updated: March 14, 2026

## Overview

Sakura ("the App") is a mobile application for streaming anime, reading manga and novels, and interacting with the Solana blockchain. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Account Data
- Solana wallet public address (used for authentication and token transactions)
- Email address (if provided during creator registration)

### Usage Data
- Reading progress and history (anime watch history, manga chapter progress, novel reading position)
- Library and favorites (saved titles, categories, bookmarks, highlights)
- Reader settings and preferences (theme, font size, TTS voice, ambient music settings)
- Search history

### Device Data
- Device type and operating system version (for compatibility)
- App version

## How We Use Your Data

- To sync your library, reading progress, settings, and bookmarks across devices
- To enable $SAKURA token transactions (chapter unlocks, creator payments, swaps)
- To display your reading history and favorites
- To improve the App's performance and user experience

## Data Storage

Your data is stored using Supabase (cloud-hosted PostgreSQL). Reading preferences and library data are also cached locally on your device for offline access.

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase** -- Cloud database for user data sync
- **Solana Blockchain** -- All token transactions are recorded on the public Solana blockchain
- **Google Books API** -- Used to fetch book cover images

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Blockchain transactions are inherently public on the Solana network.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the App. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your stored data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your data (novel bookmarks, library, reading progress)

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
